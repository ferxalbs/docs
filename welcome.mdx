## What is Rainy MaTE?

Rainy MaTE is an advanced autonomous AI assistant designed to revolutionize how you interact with your digital workspace. Built on the "Tahoe" design system, it offers a seamless, intuitive, and powerful interface for managing tasks, deploying agents, and automating workflows.

## Key Features

Rainy MaTE brings a suite of powerful tools to your desktop:

- **Autonomous Workflows**: Let Rainy MaTE handle complex, multi-step tasks without constant supervision.
- **Zero-Trust Security**: Your data remains secure with our robust security architecture.
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Available on macOS, Windows, and Linux.
- **Extensible Skill System**: Expand your agents' capabilities with a growing library of skills.
